Questions Parents & Students Ask Us Most

Yes — Gurukul Management Studies is affiliated to Maulana Abul Kalam Azad University of Technology (MAKAUT), Government of West Bengal and approved by AICTE (All India Council for Technical Education). The institution is also recognised by IIC (Institution's Innovation Council) and the Ministry of Education, Govt. of India.

The BBA programme is available as a 3-Year Degree (6 semesters) and a 4-Year Honours Degree (8 semesters), both affiliated to MAKAUT.

Candidates must have passed 10+2 (any stream — Science, Commerce, or Arts) with a minimum of 45% marks. Students currently appearing in their Class 12 examinations are also eligible to apply provisionally.

The 3-Year BBA Degree costs ₹1,74,000 (₹42,000 admission + ₹22,000 × 6 semesters). The 4-Year BBA Honours costs ₹2,18,000 (₹42,000 admission + ₹22,000 × 8 semesters), payable semester-wise. University registration and examination fees are charged separately.

Yes. Merit-based discounts are available: 15% off for students scoring above 90% in Class 12, 10% off for above 80%, and 5% off for above 70%. A 7% discount is also offered if the full course fee is paid at once. Army and Police wards are eligible for this discount. Education loan guidance is also available.

BBA graduates can pursue roles such as Business Development Executive, Marketing Manager, HR Coordinator, Finance Associate, Operations Executive, Relationship Manager, Sales Manager, and Management Trainee across corporate, banking, FMCG, retail, and startup sectors. Many graduates also pursue MBA for further specialisation.
